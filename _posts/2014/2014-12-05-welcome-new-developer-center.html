Welcome to the new Developer Center.
The Developer Center is meant to be our new home where the MobileFirst Platform team will share the following with the developer community:

  • Getting Started tutorials and samples in a fresh and web-friendly format
  • Access to our Developer Edition free downloads: the Studio plugin, Command Line Interface zip file, and the Test Workbench plugin.
  • Technical blog posts covering topics ranging from implementation tips & tricks to relevant news, updates and announcements
  • Video clips showing how to install, update, use and implement various features and technologies in native and hybrid apps
  • Help when you need it - join our community on Stack Overflow, where most developer questions can be answered, or get full product support for your production environment.

Other perks:

  • You can now also comment on individual tutorials and provide feedback!
  • We have provided better organizing and filtering capability to allow you to see just what you want to see

So take a look around, and we hope you'll enjoy this new Developer Center.
